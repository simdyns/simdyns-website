Engineering Analysis 2021

Modified Drive Train Torsional Analysis

Turbine Efficiency — United Kingdom

Torsional vibration analysis of a modified combined drive train of a Siemens SGT-100-1S Gas Turbine, performed in accordance with API Recommended Practice for Packaged Gas Turbines.

Key Activities

  • Computerised analysis of undamped torsional resonances (critical speeds)
  • Campbell Diagram with critical speed plots and torsional excitation mapping
  • Technical report with conclusions per API norms

  • Injection mould tooling fabrication and production operations
  • Full contracted quantity delivered
