Engineering Analysis 2021

Modified Drive Train Torsional Analysis

Turbine Efficiency — United Kingdom

Torsional vibration analysis of a modified combined drive train of a Siemens SGT-100-1S Gas Turbine, performed in accordance with API Recommended Practice for Packaged Gas Turbines.

Key Activities

  • Computerised analysis of undamped torsional resonances and critical speeds
  • Campbell Diagram with critical speed plots and excitation mapping (1×, 2×, 3× per revolution)
  • Technical report with conclusions and recommendations per API norms

Product Development 2022–2024

Redesign of Nozzle for Dust Control Equipment

ASEC Environmental Protection (ASENPRO) — Egypt

Material substitution and redesign of a dust control nozzle — changing from die-cast aluminium to Fiberglass Reinforced Polyamide (Nylon) via injection moulding, achieving significant cost reduction without compromising functional performance.

Key Activities

  • Geometrical characterisation from original metallic part sample
  • Material selection study and new design verification documentation
  • Engineering documentation for the injection moulding production process
  • Injection mould tooling fabrication and full production operations
  • Full contracted quantity delivered to specification
