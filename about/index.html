About

Multidisciplinary Engineering Competences

SimDyns specialises in energy management solutions fully compliant with ISO 50001 and ISO 50005 guidelines, working alongside organisations to build robust energy performance frameworks that deliver lasting cost savings and regulatory compliance. Our expert team brings international standards to practical implementation — whether that means commissioning an energy monitoring platform, redesigning a production process, or qualifying an organisation for certification.

Through targeted Deep Tech training curricula — covering computational simulation, embedded systems, AI applications, and advanced engineering principles — we empower clients across the energy, aerospace, automotive, industrial machinery, home appliances, and steel construction sectors to build in-house technological capabilities and achieve sustainable competitive advantages.

Our Approach

  • High competences with competitive prices and commitment to deadlines.
  • Lean process with predefined milestones and gateways.
  • Quality mindset with a set of reviews processes and quality control and assurance.
  • Facilitating a problem-solving oriented approach, applied fundamentally to all activities regardless of specific branches of industry.
  • Encouraging inventive and cognitive skills, to ease identification of the optimum solution in each case.
  • Cross-disciplinary thinking applied to every engagement.
  • Generating solutions that are systematic rather than ad hoc.
  • Designing solutions transferable to related problems and domains.
  • Leveraging digital engineering tools and simulation platforms throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Developing teachable, documentable, and scalable solutions.
  • Human-centred design principles embedded throughout our processes — simplifying tasks, saving time, and enabling effective collaboration.
  • Proven product development methods including Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) and structured process modelling.
