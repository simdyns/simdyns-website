SimDyns Launches AI-Powered Cybersecurity Training Program Aligned with EIT Deep Tech Goals

EIT Deep Tech Training and SimDyns Contribution as Pledger

SimDyns Technology Solutions has launched an innovative AI-powered Cybersecurity Training Program, aimed at advancing skills in the deep tech sector. This program is designed to equip participants with essential knowledge and practical skills to navigate the complexities of cybersecurity in an increasingly digital world.

By leveraging artificial intelligence, the training offers a dynamic learning experience that prepares individuals to tackle modern cyber threats effectively.

This initiative aligns with broader efforts to enhance deep tech capabilities across Europe, contributing to the goal of training 1 million EU residents in deep tech fields by the end of 2025. SimDyns is committed to fostering a skilled workforce that can meet the challenges of tomorrow's technology landscape.
