SimDyns Launches Updated Energy Management Platform with Enhanced Monitoring and Analytics

Energy Management · Digital Platform · ISO 50001

SimDyns Technology Solutions is pleased to announce the launch of its significantly updated Energy Management Platform — a web-based solution purpose-built for industrial organisations seeking to monitor, analyse, and optimise their energy consumption in real time.

The updated platform brings a range of new and enhanced capabilities, building on the foundation developed under the EU SeeBridge grant programme. It is designed to support full ISO 50001 compliance and provides operational teams with the tools they need to drive continuous energy performance improvement.

What's New in the Updated Platform

  • Enhanced Real-Time Monitoring: Improved dashboards delivering live visibility into energy consumption across multiple sites, systems, and equipment categories.
  • Advanced Energy Performance Indicators (EnPIs): Configurable KPIs aligned with ISO 50001, enabling teams to track efficiency trends and benchmark performance over time.
  • Significant Energy Use (SEU) Management: Dedicated modules to identify, monitor, and manage the systems and processes with the highest energy impact.
  • Forecasting and Anomaly Detection: Data-driven forecasting tools that flag abnormal consumption patterns and support proactive intervention before costs escalate.
  • Improved Reporting Suite: Automated report generation for management reviews, regulatory submissions, and sustainability disclosures.
  • Multi-User Access and Role Management: Granular access controls allowing energy managers, engineers, and executives to work within a shared platform at appropriate permission levels.

The platform is accessible via any modern web browser with no installation required, making it straightforward to deploy across facilities of any scale.
